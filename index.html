Frequently Asked Questions

Is ROZE safe for children? +

Yes. ROZE is specifically formulated to be safe if swallowed — food-grade ingredients only, no fluoride toxicity risk. Many customers introduce it to children as young as 18 months.

How long until I see results? +

Reduced sensitivity within 14 days. Visible whitening typically between weeks 3 and 6. Enamel remineralization is continuous — the longer you use it, the stronger your teeth become.

Do I need to rinse after brushing? +

No. In fact, do not rinse. Letting the HA stay on your teeth maximizes remineralization.

Does it actually replace fluoride? +

Yes — and arguably better. Fluoride creates a thin coating. Hydroxyapatite IS the mineral your enamel is made of, so it doesn't just coat — it rebuilds. Japan has used HA as its national standard since 1980.

Can I use it with braces or veneers? +

Yes to both. HA is non-abrasive and biocompatible with all dental work — braces, veneers, crowns, implants.
